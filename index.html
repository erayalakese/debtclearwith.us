Payoff Strategies

Snowball or Avalanche?
DebtClear calculates both.

The best plan is the one you'll stick with. DebtClear shows you what each approach looks like for your specific debts — with real numbers.

Debt Snowball

Start Small. Build Momentum.

Pay minimums on everything, then put every extra dollar toward your smallest balance first. When that's paid off, roll that payment to the next one.

  • Quick early wins keep motivation high
  • Reduces the number of creditors faster
  • Proven effective for behavioral reasons
Debt Avalanche

Attack the Highest Rate First.

Pay minimums everywhere, then focus extra payments on the debt with the highest interest rate. Minimizes total interest in the long run.

  • Minimizes total estimated interest paid
  • Fastest payoff timeline mathematically
  • Best when keeping motivated isn't the challenge
